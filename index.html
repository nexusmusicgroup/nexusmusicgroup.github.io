Who are we?

Nexus Music Group is a partnership by Darrell Alman and Olly Wiggins specialising in management for artists based in the Guildford and Clapham campuses of ACM. We work closely with Discovery & Development as well as our artists to guide them through an ever-changing; ever-growing industry. With knowledge and experience in live, content creation and distribution (just to name a few!) we believe we offer fantastic services for artists taking their first steps into the industry.
